Famish

The Jetlag Fasting Calculator

Stop letting your vacations be ruined by jetlag! Our bodies can have our internal clock's reset by fasting and Famish does all the time zone conversions for you.

If you stop eating for 10 to 12 hours, the moment you break your fast becomes your body's new morning time. Using this technique you can hack your body's clock when you travel to new time zones.

How It Works

  1. Pick your departure timezone.
  2. Pick your arrival timezone.
  3. Pick your arrival time.
