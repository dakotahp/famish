Frequently Asked Questions

Does the fasting technique really work?

Yes, it is almost magical traveling to your foreign destination and having your body completely adjust to a new time zone instantly.

Is fasting dangerous?

Fasting has been practiced for health and religious reasons for thousands of years but consult a medical professional before fasting for such a long period of time. It may be dangerous to operate motor vehicles if you are adversely affected by Hypoglycemia during the fasting period.

How much does Famish cost?

You may use Famish for free to determine your fasting schedule. An in-app purchase for $0.99 is available to upgrade the app to a Pro version which will create calendar alerts for when to start and stop fasting.
